The Manhattan Project
The Manhattan Project was one of the most ambitious scientific efforts in history. Beginning in 1942, it brought together thousands of physicists, engineers, and military people who worked in secrecy to develop the world's first atomic weapons. This project combined theoretical physics with large-scale industrial engineering, creating entirely new technologies in record time. Together, these developments show how a collection of great minds and experiments came together and transformed into one of the largest discoveries in history.
