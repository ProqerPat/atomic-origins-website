Discovering Nuclear Fission

Many early 20th-century scientists worked to combine theoretical pysics with experiments to unlock the atom's secrets. Their work laid the foundation for both scientific breakthroughs and the development of the atomic bomb, forever changing the way humanity understood energy and matter.
